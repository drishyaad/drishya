About Me

I am Drishya Adhikari, a dedicated IT student at Gandaki University and the president of the All Nepal National Independent Students' Union (Revolutionary) [ANNISU(R)] Gandaki University National Committee. My passion lies in leadership, advocacy, and student empowerment, as I work diligently to foster a progressive and inclusive academic environment.

As a student leader, I aim to be a voice for my peers, advocating for constructive changes and contributing to education and social development through effective measures. My approach combines collaboration and championing student rights through activities that catalyze positive change.

I firmly believe that activism and education are complementary forces that can create a better future for students and society at large. I invite you to join me as we build a more empowered and vibrant academic community together.
