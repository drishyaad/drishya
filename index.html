About Me

I am Drishya Adhikari, a diligent IT student at Gandaki University and the president of the All Nepal National Independent Students' Union (Revolutionary) Gandaki University National Committee [ANNISU(R)]. I have a strong passion for leadership, lobbying, and empowering students and actively work to create a progressive and inclusive academic environment. I strive to become a voice for students, advocating constructive changes, and contributing to education and social development through effective measures.

>Through my work, I aim to promote collaboration, champion the rights of students, and become involved in activities that lead to positive change. I am convinced that activism and education complement each other as they develop a better future for students and society. Come along with me as we build a more empowered and vibrant academic community together.
